# kinetix-flow
Transforming urban transportation through predictive analytics. Kinetix uses Machine Learning to forecast bus occupancy and transit delays, providing a seamless navigation experience for commuters and data-driven insights for authorities.
🚌 Kinetix: Making Urban Transit Actually Work
We’ve all been there—standing at a bus stop in Baku, watching a packed bus go by, and wondering if the next one will be any better or if we should just give up and call a taxi.

Kinetix was born out of that frustration. We aren't just building another "bus tracker." We’re building a system that understands the city’s pulse to make your daily commute predictable, comfortable, and—dare we say—actually pleasant.

🛑 The "Baku Commute" Problem
Current navigation apps are great at showing you the shortest path, but they’re blind to the human experience:

The Guessing Game: You know the bus is 2 minutes away, but you don't know if you'll be able to fit through the door.

The "Right Now" Trap: Apps show you current traffic, but they can't tell you that in 15 minutes, the road you’re heading toward will be a parking lot.

The Chaos: Without real-time data, city authorities can’t react fast enough when a specific route gets overwhelmed.

✨ Our Fix: Think Ahead, Not Just Now
Kinetix changes the way we move by looking into the near future. Instead of just giving you a route, it gives you a choice:

"Hey, Bus #1 is arriving in 4 minutes, but it's 95% full. If you wait 10 minutes, Bus #2 will be here—it's nearly empty, and you'll actually get a seat. Plus, you’ll save 15 minutes of standing in a crowd."

Behind the scenes, we also provide a Heatmap for transport authorities (like AYNA). It's a "red alert" system that says: "It's 6:00 PM and this line is drowning—send an extra bus now."

📊 Where do we get the data?
We don’t guess. We connect the dots between:

Live Feeds: Real-time GPS and BakıKart tap-in data to see exactly how many people are on the move.

Street Intel: Live traffic flow from open sources to predict delays before they happen.

Memory: Our system learns from the past—how rain, Mondays, or big events change the way the city breathes.

💼 More Than Just a Social Project
Kinetix is a sustainable business designed to help everyone win:

For the City: We help authorities save fuel and manage their fleet like a pro.

For Businesses: We provide an API for delivery giants (Wolt, Trendyol) so their couriers can dodge the "future" traffic.

For Local Life: Stuck waiting for a bus? We’ll point you to a nearby cafe for a discounted coffee while the crowd clears out.

🛠 Tech Talk
We keep it lean and fast:

Core: Python (FastAPI)

Intelligence: Predictive models for time-series and density forecasting.

Visuals: Real-time dashboards for city-scale monitoring.
